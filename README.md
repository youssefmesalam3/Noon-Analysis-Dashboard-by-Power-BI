# 🛒 Noon Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The Noon Sales Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, product categories, item ratings, salesperson contributions, and monthly sales trends.

The dashboard provides valuable business insights into product performance, customer preferences, sales distribution, and revenue growth, helping stakeholders make data-driven decisions and improve business performance.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Monitor overall sales performance.
- Analyze product category contributions.
- Identify top-selling products.
- Evaluate product ratings and popularity.
- Track salesperson performance.
- Monitor monthly sales trends.
- Support data-driven business decisions.

## 🔍 Key Insights

### Sales Performance
- Total sales exceeded **627K**.
- More than **20K units** were sold.
- The average selling price reached **1.18K**.

### Product Analysis
- A small number of product groups generated a significant share of total sales.
- Product rating analysis helped identify the most popular items.

### Category Performance
- Local products generated the largest share of sales.
- Product category analysis revealed key revenue-driving segments.

### Salesperson Performance
- One salesperson contributed over 70% of total sales.
- Sales performance varied significantly across sales representatives.

### Monthly Trends
- June recorded the highest sales performance.
- Strong sales performance continued during July.
- Sales activity fluctuated across the analyzed months.
